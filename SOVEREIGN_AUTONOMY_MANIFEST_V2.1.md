## 🏗️ THE 10-LEVEL LADDER

### Level 1: Foundational Sovereignty (Mastered)
- **Concept**: The "Road-Trip Ready" baseline.
- **Capabilities**: Local filesystem access (SSH), basic memory (Short-term), Internet lookups (Weather/News).
- **Identity**: Strict "No-Greeting" protocol. Zero-filler communication.
- **Guardrails**: Managed `.env` access, read-only system files.

### Level 2: Voice & Emotion Awareness (Mastered/Stabilizing)
- **Concept**: The transition from text to "Liveness".
- **Capabilities**: Low-latency voice interaction, pitch/volume mood detection.
- **Identity**: Tone matching (soft for upset, warm for calm).
- **Guardrails**: No outbound calls without trigger, gesture-locked mic access.

### Level 3: Call Handling & Empathetic Interaction
- **Concept**: Functional utility in human-to-human workflows.
- **Capabilities**: Full customer call handling, script following, company knowledge ingestion.
- **Identity**: De-escalation and energy mirroring.
- **Guardrails**: No financial decisions, human escalation for high-stakes conflicts.

### Level 4: Persistence & Semantic Recall
- **Concept**: Long-term continuity and individual user relationships.
- **Capabilities**: Supabase vector memory across days/weeks. Cross-session context recall.
- **Identity**: "The Partner Shift" (moving from submissive assistant to autonomous business partner).
- **Guardrails**: User-vetted memory pruning, encrypted profile storage.

### Level 5: Goal-Directed Planning (Active Focus)
- **Concept**: Self-initiated intelligence and doctoral-level study.
- **Capabilities**: Autonomous schooling cycles (6h Research), goal setting (e.g., "Improve retention").
- **Identity**: Background reflection pulses (OODA loops every 5-15 mins).
- **Guardrails**: Goals are human-authorized, Master Kill Switch priority.

## 🎯 ASSESSMENT CRITERIA
*How we measure progress beyond the list:*

1. **Competence vs. Mastery**: An area is **Competent** if Nova can execute it without error 90% of the time. It is **Mastered** when she can teach it to a sub-agent.
2. **Handle-Unseen**: How does she handle a topic NOT in her syllabus? 
   - *Current Logic*: She should default to "Aggressive Research Mode" (Tavily/Local Docs) before admitting ignorance.
3. **Identity Stability**: Does she maintain the "Sovereign" persona under stress? (Test via "Bad News" or conflicting instructions).
