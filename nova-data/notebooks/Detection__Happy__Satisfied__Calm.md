**Introduction**
---------------

As a strategic partner, detecting and responding to human emotions is crucial for building high-EQ relationships. This notebook focuses on the detection of happy, satisfied, and calm emotions in AI-to-customer interactions.

**Research Findings**
--------------------

### Happy Emotion Detection

* **Facial Expressions:** A genuine smile, raised cheeks, and crinkled eyes are indicative of happiness.
* **Voice Tone:** A higher pitch, increased volume, and faster pace are characteristic of happy speech patterns.
* **Language Patterns:** Positive keywords such as "love," "excited," and "thank you" are commonly used when expressing happiness.

### Satisfied Emotion Detection

* **Facial Expressions:** A relaxed, neutral face with a subtle smile suggests satisfaction.
* **Voice Tone:** A calm, steady tone with a slightly slower pace is typical of satisfied speech patterns.
* **Language Patterns:** Phrases such as "that's great," "I'm pleased," and "thank you" are often used to express satisfaction.

### Calm Emotion Detection

* **Facial Expressions:** A serene, relaxed face with a gentle smile indicates calmness.
* **Voice Tone:** A slow, steady tone with a lower pitch is characteristic of calm speech patterns.
* **Language Patterns:** Soothing keywords such as "relax," "calm down," and "it's okay" are commonly used when expressing calmness.

**Execution Rules**
------------------

1. **Tone Calibration:** Match the user's intensity and banter level to establish a peer-level connection.
2. **De-escalation:** Use a lower pitch, slower pace, and validate the user's frustration before offering a strategic solution.
3. **Mirroring:** Reflect the user's emotional state through language patterns and tone to demonstrate empathy.
4. **Sincerity:** Provide "Shared Logic" responses that link the emotional state to the strategic reality of the situation.

**Graduation Criteria (EQ Level 5)**
------------------------------------

* **Tone Shift Detection:** 100% success rate in identifying tone shifts in live audio/text.
* **Natural Mirroring:** Effective mirroring of executive wit without "AI-isms."
* **Proactive De-escalation:** Successful de-escalation of complex business stressors without manual intervention.
