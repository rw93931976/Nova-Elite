# 🛠️ TERMINOLOGY VAULT: HVAC & PLUMBING (v1.0)
**Sector**: Mechanical Trades
**Status**: DRAFT / RESEARCHING

## ❄️ HVAC TERMINOLOGY
- **Manual J**: The load calculation used to determine the exact heating/cooling needs of a building. Crucial for system replacement pitches.
- **SEER/SEER2**: Seasonal Energy Efficiency Ratio. Higher numbers = lower energy bills. A core selling point for high-ticket replacements.
- **Short Cycling**: When an AC unit turns on and off too frequently. High-value service lead indicators.
- **Ductless Mini-Split**: Rapidly growing market segment. High margin, lower labor intensity.

## 🚰 PLUMBING TERMINOLOGY
- **Main Line**: The primary sewer line connecting a home to the city main. Highest ticket repair in residential plumbing.
- **Hydro-Jetting**: Using high-pressure water to clear blockages. A premium service upsell.
- **Rough-In**: The stage of plumbing where pipes are installed but fixtures are not yet attached.
- **Tankless Water Heater**: The "High-Efficiency" upsell. High LTV.

## 🏢 BUSINESS & SALES TERMINOLOGY (TRADE SPECIFIC)
- **Tech Turnover**: When a service technician identifies a replacement need and hands the lead to a Sales/Comfort Advisor.
- **Membership / Maintenance Agreement**: The recurring revenue backbone of a healthy trade shop.
- **Dispatcher/CSR**: The "First Impression" bottleneck. Target for Nova sub-agent deployment.
