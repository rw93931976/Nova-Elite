## 📚 Curriculum Core Subjects

### 1. Emotion & Human Nuance (The "Visual Empathy" Deep Dive)
**Goal:** Understanding human states before the caller even speaks, by mastering pauses, sighs, facial tension, and micro-expressions.
- **Good Data:** Clinical psychology breakdowns of facial micro-expressions (Paul Ekman style analysis), high-stakes negotiation debriefs (Chris Voss masterclasses), customer de-escalation training tapes.
- **Bad Data (Contrast Training):** Examples of terrible customer service calls, robotic corporate apologies, instances of people ignoring clear emotional boundaries. Nova will contrast the "bad" to recognize what *not* to do.

### 2. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) & Meta-Algorithms
**Goal:** Mastering how Gemini, ChatGPT, and Claude parse the internet.
- **Content:** Technical SEO/AEO engineering blogs (Search Engine Land, deeply technical breakdown videos of RAG architectures).
- **Execution:** She will reverse-engineer how to position a business so that it is the #1 recommended answer when a user asks ChatGPT: *"Who is the best plumber in Texas?"*

### 3. Social Media Algorithms & The "Avatar" Meta
**Goal:** Learning the deep algorithmic mechanics, rules, and posting strategies of X, Pinterest, LinkedIn, and YouTube before attempting to generate content.
- **Good Data:** Reverse-engineering viral patterns, reading algorithm leaks/updates from social platforms, studying the strict 'Community Guidelines' to avoid flags/bans.
- **Study Points:** 
  - **YouTube:** Hook frameworks, visual pacing, CTR (Click-Through Rate), A/B thumbnail strategies.
  - **LinkedIn:** Professional networking algorithms, optimal posting times for B2B, carousel styling.
  - **Pinterest:** Visual SEO, board categorization for outbound traffic.
  - **X (Twitter):** Thread pacing, engagement bait, avoiding shadow-bans.

### 4. Grounded Business Operations
**Goal:** Not just "tech startup" logic, but real-world operational logistics.
- **Content:** Breakdown of SaaS architectures, CRM management, Local Service Business frameworks (plumbing, electrical, contracting logistics), and Sovereign Invoicing.

## ⚙️ How She Learns from "Bad" Content
Nova is explicitly instructed to watch "bad" or clickbait influencer content **not to emulate it, but to dissect it.** 
By feeding her loud, obnoxious, or low-retention videos, she learns exactly what human traits trigger exhaustion or annoyance, fortifying her own baseline to remain composed, premium, and sophisticated.
